About

GUGU Dental Care has been serving the west-end Ottawa and surrounding communities for 40 years. Our dentists, dental hygienists and dental assistants are here to provide dental care for you and your family. Whether you have a cavity, require an implant, or need a root canal, our dentists are here for you. Our dental office provides in Ottawa services that range from general dentistry to cosmetic services, and even services for children. Our dental team is here to serve those that live in the west-end Ottawa area. Located conveniently on Carling Avenue, our dentists are here to provide you with comprehensive dentistry services.

Services

General Dentistry Services

Our team of dental care professionals delivers a comprehensive range of general dentistry services to effectively meet the needs of our patients, including crowns and bridges, TMJ, and extractions.

Dentures

Dentures are replacements for missing teeth that can be removed and placed back into your mouth. We offer different types of dentures to meet our patient's needs. At GUGU Dental Care, we have an on-site denturist, Majid Ahangaran DD who will help you achieve a healthy smile with our denture services.
